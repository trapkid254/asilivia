About Asilivia Tech

Our Story

Asilivia Tech was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality electronics and reliable repair services to our community. What started as a small repair shop has grown into a comprehensive technology solution provider.

Our Mission

We strive to make technology accessible and affordable for everyone. Whether you're looking for the latest smartphone, need your laptop repaired, or want advice on the best gadgets for your needs, we're here to help.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the best parts and products
  • Expertise: Our team is knowledgeable and certified
  • Customer Service: Your satisfaction is our priority
  • Innovation: We stay up-to-date with the latest technology
